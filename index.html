ABOUT ME

Hi! I'm Jhoelyn Mire N. Tan

For this blog, I want to share some random facts and hobbies of mine, from quirky interests to unexpected skills I've picked up over the years. Whether it’s the odd things I collect, the random projects I dive into, or the habits I’ve developed, each one gives me a unique perspective on life. It’s a fun way to reflect on the little things that make life interesting, and hopefully, it’ll inspire you to try something new or rediscover a forgotten passion of your own.

Why she loves exploring?

She loves exploring new things and picking up a variety of hobbies. Her curiosity keeps her constantly learning and trying new experiences.

At the same time, she knows her strengths and focuses on mastering what truly excites her. This balance of exploration and expertise makes her both dynamic and accomplished.
